YOUNG GLORY 2018/2019 - SEPTEMBER BRIEF

"How would you get people across the globe, in particular teenagers, to start searching the term skateboarding?"

— Sam Keene

INSIGHT

Skateboarding is a form of self expression just like art and streetwear.

OPPORTUNITY

So what if we create tangible value in skateboarding just art and streetwear?

SOLUTION

SOLUTION

SKATE
MARKET

In collaboration with StockX, we setup the world’s first ever skate market, where skaters can sell their stunts to people who would like to learn them.

Like a stock market, one can bid how much he is going to pay to get a lesson and the other can choose how much he’s going to charge to teach.
